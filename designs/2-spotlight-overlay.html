Here's a command to find files larger than 10MB in your repo:
bash
find . -type f -size +10M -not -path './.git/*' \
| xargs ls -lhS
Or to also check what git is tracking that's large:
bash
git rev-list --objects --all \
| git cat-file --batch-check='%(objectsize) %(rest)' \
| sort -rn | head -20
